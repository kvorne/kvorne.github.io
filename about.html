    I Believe Great Design is

    Intentional
    Every decision I make has an impact on the user experience and therefore must be informed by research, empathy, and expertise.

    Compassionate
    I’m not just designing for users. I’m designing for people. With each new thing I learn about them, I am more driven to deliver empowering solutions.

    Collaborative
    I democratize design, using tools to lift the voices of cross-functional teams and potential users, leading to better outcomes for the business and users.

    How I got Here and Where I’m Going

    Looking for the power to make a positive impact on peoples’ daily lives, I decided to study Software Engineering. Unfortunately, I often felt out of place. While my classmates were asking how to run a SQL query, I was asking which database type would give an experience of consistency and security.

    It wasn’t until I took courses in Human Computer Interaction and Design Thinking that I felt I was making the most of the curiosity and compassion that brought me to Tech in the first place. I felt at home working together with people of all academic backgrounds towards the common goals of understanding and serving the needs of others.

    Nowadays, once I’ve closed my small army of Adobe apps, I spend my time singing, sketching, and meandering, with and without my adorable Alaskan Klee Kai, taking in the weird and wonderful world around me.

    For my next step, I’m looking to contribute to and learn from a passionate, collaborative, and cross-functional team that values research and empathy-informed, intentional design. If this sounds like you, I’d love to connect at Hello@kvorne.com !
